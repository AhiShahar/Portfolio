About Me:

Hello! I'm Ahi, a Sydney based web developer, who just finished a 12 weeks immersive course at General Assembly.

I first discovered a passion for software development in computer science during high school. Since then with 3 years in the IDF and 7 years as a baker since arriving in Australia, I have finally decided it is time to follow my passion. That passion has been recently reignited through a life changing decision to completely immerse myself into full time web development study at General Assembly, and all that worked pretty well with my secret life as a cave diver who loves to push his limits and explore new realms!

In the past weeks of study, I have learnt various languages, libraries and frameworks such as JavaScript, Ruby, jQuery, Rails, Backbone, React, D3js, Ajax, and have touched on several others. I have also developed my skills in teamwork, learning how to read and debug other developer's work.

I have also honed to my natural curiosity to understand how things work and how/if they can be improved. This focus has allowed me to quickly develop a greater understanding of web development in such a short time and has encouraged me to study simultaneously online, using sites such as Wes Bos's online courses.

I am continuously pushing myself to strive for the best and further myself as a person and web developer, the current challange is writing this site, and the task is to do it using only my own code. Hope you'll enjoy it!
